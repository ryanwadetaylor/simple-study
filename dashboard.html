Metaphysics

It is not easy to say what metaphysics is. Ancient and Medieval philosophers might have said that metaphysics was to be defined by its subject matter: metaphysics was the “science” that studied “being as such” or “the first causes of things” or “things that do not change”.

Epistemology

Epistemology is about issues having to do with the creation and dissemination of knowledge in particular areas of inquiry.

Anthropology

This area of inquiry examines the basic elements that comprise the human being, how these elements can promote knowing about truth and arriving at wisdom

Philosophy of Religion

Philosophy of religion is the philosophical examination of the central themes and concepts involved in religious traditions. It involves all the main areas of philosophy: metaphysics, epistemology, logic, ethics, and so on.

Ethics

The field of ethics (or moral philosophy) involves systematizing, defending, and recommending concepts of right and wrong behavior.
